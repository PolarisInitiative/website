What is Polaris Initiative?

Polaris is a non-profit initiative taken by a group of youth, aiming to provide career guidance to Grade-12 students to help them find the major that suits them best, both their skills or abilities and their ambitions or dreams (what they actually like!).
Polaris, the northern star, guides people to their way; same for our teams, hoping to guide students to a shiny bright career!
